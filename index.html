Our Story

Sammy and I first met each other at an Asian Intervarsity Fellowship group at the University of Michigan. It was at Fall Retreat, where we both fell head over heels for each other. Just kidding ;) Our story didn't have such a rosy beginning, but some might say it's one of perseverance.

During our college years, our relationship grew as friends and eventually as partners in crime ... despite being 2000 miles away. We aren’t sure why we thought long distance out of college would be a good idea, but looking back on it, we certainly feel blessed to have had the opportunity to grow together from afar. We learned to communicate and trust each other and became video call connoisseurs even before the pandemic. Six years passed, and now I am in Chicago to marry the love of my life.

- Jay

FAQ

How do I know who is invited? (Children? Plus ones?)

We have limited space at our venues and so have a hard cap for guest counts! You can see who is registered from your party when you RSVP including plus ones. If you are traveling from out of town, we included your children in your invite!

What if my RSVP details have changed? Can I resubmit it?

Resubmit it as many times as you’d like! We will only use the latest response.

What COVID protocols will be in place?

The safety of our family and friends is our top priority. We, along with our vendors, will be monitoring COVID-19 safety protocols for the city of Chicago. Currently, Chicago requires masks indoors, when not eating or drinking, and proof of vaccinations for large events and at restaurants. Should anything change we will promptly communicate.

What is the dress code?

The ceremony and reception will be hosted indoors. The dress code is daytime semi-formal.

What is the timeline for the day?

The ceremony will start promptly at 10:00 AM at The Arbory, 2219 West Grand Avenue. Following the ceremony, we will have a brunch reception at The Dawson, 730 West Grand Avenue. The celebrations will conclude by 2:00 pm.

How do I get around? What is parking and transportation like at the venues?

Both the ceremony, at the Arbory in West Town, and reception at The Dawson in the West Loop, are located centrally in the city of Chicago. By car, they are about 10 minutes from the downtown area, known as the Loop.
If possible, we recommend carpooling or using car-share services.

Ceremony parking at The Arbory: Free street parking is available along Grand Avenue as well as all side streets around The Arbory.
Reception parking at The Dawson: Complimentary valet street parking is available at the restaurant. Metered street parking is available on Halsted Avenue, Grand Avenue, and Milwaukee Avenue.

Will there be a virtual version of the wedding?

In the early spring, we will assess RSVPs and COVID safety protocols. If appropriate, we will determine a virtual plan for the wedding and provide more information on the website. If you are unable to make the wedding, we promise to share lots of photos and videos after the event!

Where is your registry?

We have a registered Zola account with a few larger items. Feel free to contribute to the larger items or to our newlywed fund. In order to avoid the transaction fee charged by Zola, you can also directly send your gift to our Zelle account at samanthaesuh@gmail.com

I still have some unanswered questions. Who can I contact to learn more?

Please feel free to email our wedding coordinator, Becca with any unanswered questions regarding the event.
